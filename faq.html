Why a Golden Retriever?

They're incredibly loyal, gentle and compassionate, which makes them wonderful family dogs. The American Kennel Club ranks them in the top 5 dogs to own as your family pet. But they're more than just a pet, they really are part of the family, they're so loving and get along great with kids. It's hard not to want to run up and pet them the moment you see them.

Golden Retrievers are incredibly intelligent, which makes them great companions. They're very obedient and the combination of intelligence and obedience makes them one of the easiest dog breeds to train.

Golden Retrievers aren't hard to take care of, though they do shed a lot! Even with proper brushing and bathing, you will have to dust mop!



FAQ

How often do you have puppies?


Rosie and Izzy have at most 2 litters every year, you can find their due dates posted on our Litters and Purchase Agreements page whenever that information is available!

What sort of conditions are the puppies kept in while they're growing up?


Our puppies are part of our family and we treat them that way. They're kept in a clean, healthy environment and given regular veterinary care and checkups until they leave our home to join yours.

Can I come see the puppies?


You sure can! After they're 6 weeks old, they can have visitors, you can head over to the Contact Us page or give me a call at 417-417-4117 to schedule an appointment!
